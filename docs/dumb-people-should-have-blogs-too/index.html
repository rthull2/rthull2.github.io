Dumb People Should Have Blogs Too.

Robert Hull

28 Nov 2021 • 1 min read

Why would anyone listen to you talk about things you don't fully understand that you learned from someone else?

Millions of people would consider you an expert, millions of people like your personality and trust people like you. There is an endless amounts of useless information. There is a bunch of useful information most people can't find or understand. You can be a part of the pipeline from the people who can find and understand the useful information to those that can't.
